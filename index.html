Core Features

Instant Notes

Create sticky notes on any webpage with a single click. Perfect for quick thoughts, reminders, and to-do lists.

Seamless Sync

Your notes are saved and synced to the specific page you created them on. Revisit the page, and your notes will be right there.

Personalize Everything

Choose from a variety of colors, resize your notes, and arrange them however you like. Make your workspace truly yours.

Drawing Mode

Sometimes typing isn't enough. Sketch out ideas, diagrams, or just doodle with the built-in drawing feature.

AI-Powered Summaries

Highlight text in a note and let our AI summarize it for you. Get the gist of long-form content in seconds.

Central Dashboard

View and manage all your notes from every website in one organized and easy-to-navigate dashboard.
